What You'll Learn

✓ Develop a strategic leadership mindset for complex challenges
✓ Build and lead high-performing executive teams

Course Description

This comprehensive masterclass is designed for mid-to-senior level professionals who want to elevate their strategic leadership capabilities. Drawing from 20 years of executive coaching experience, Dr. Sarah Mitchell delivers a curriculum that combines theoretical frameworks with practical, real-world applications.

Through case studies from leading organizations, interactive exercises, and peer discussions, you'll develop the skills necessary to lead with confidence and drive meaningful organizational transformation.

About

Dr. Sarah Mitchell is a renowned executive leadership coach with over 20 years of experience in corporate leadership and organizational development. Previously serving as Vice President of Strategy at a Fortune 500 technology company, she brings a unique blend of academic rigor and real-world experience to her teaching.

She holds a Ph.D. in Organizational Leadership from Harvard University and an MBA from Wharton. Her research on strategic leadership decision-making has been published in the Harvard Business Review, MIT Sloan Management Review, and the Academy of Management Journal.
